What to expect

Sunday, in four small details.

No dress code. No insider lingo. No pressure. Just a warm room, real songs, an honest message, and people who want to know your name.

About 70 minutes

Worship music, an honest message, and a moment to respond. Doors open 30 minutes before each service.

Free parking, easy entry

Look for our team in orange vests. They'll save you a great spot and walk you straight to the welcome center.

Kids will love it

Birth–5th grade have age-appropriate environments with secure check-in. Trained leaders, every weekend.

Come as you are

Jeans, suits, sweats, scrubs — all welcome. Whatever you're carrying today, you don't have to carry it alone.

What we hold
We believe the gospel is good news before it's good advice — and that the same grace that welcomes us is the grace that grows us.
— Our shared confession · written 2024

Scripture, with humility

The Bible is our authority — and we read it together, slowly, in community, with the church across history.

Jesus, at the center

Every page, every prayer, every program is finally about him. If it's not, we'd rather drop it.

Spirit, not strategy

We can plan and prepare — and then we have to depend. We pray as if it actually matters, because it does.

People, not projects

You are not a metric. You are not a target audience. You are someone Jesus loves. That changes everything.
